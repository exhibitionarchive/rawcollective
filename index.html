原质社 RAW COLLECTIVE

About

Raw Collective is an interdisciplinary design research group. Its members come from diverse media and disciplines, including creative media studies, art and crafts practice, and space design. We are united by a shared belief: strip away the superfluous, return to the essential. Through the act of making, we explore the power of materials, space, and new media to speak directly to the human spirit.

關於

原质社是一个跨领域的设计研究小组，成员来自创意媒体研究、工艺美术实践、建筑及室内设计等多个媒介及方向。团队成员因共同的信念而聚集：剥离浮饰，回归本真，在材料、空间、新媒介之中，以造物行为探索那些直指人心的力量。
